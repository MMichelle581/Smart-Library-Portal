Microbiology BIOT F212 - Exam Paper 2

Part A: Microbial Structure (25 marks)

1. Explain the bacterial cell wall structure.
2. Describe bacterial flagella and motility.

7. What are disinfectants and antiseptics?

9. Discuss antimicrobial resistance.
10. What is the concept of MIC and MBC?

Part C: Immunology (40 marks)

11. Explain innate and adaptive immunity.
12. Describe antigen-antibody interactions.
13. What are vaccines? Discuss types.
14. Explain hypersensitivity reactions.
15. Discuss autoimmune diseases.
