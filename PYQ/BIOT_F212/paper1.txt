Microbiology BIOT F212 - Exam Paper 1
Time: 3 Hours                                                    Max Marks: 100

Section A: Microbial Diversity (30 marks)

1. Describe the classification of microorganisms.
2. Explain bacterial cell structure and function.
3. What are viruses? Discuss their structure and replication.
4. Compare bacteria, archaea, and eukarya.
5. Discuss fungal morphology and reproduction.

Section B: Microbial Physiology (35 marks)

6. Explain microbial growth and factors affecting it.
7. Describe different culture media and techniques.
8. What is microbial metabolism? Discuss pathways.
9. Explain fermentation processes.
10. Discuss microbial genetics and gene transfer.

Section C: Applied Microbiology (35 marks)

11. Explain industrial applications of microorganisms.
12. Describe food microbiology and preservation.
13. What is medical microbiology? Discuss pathogens.
14. Explain environmental microbiology.
15. Discuss bioremediation and its applications.
