International Law HSS F252 - Exam Paper 2
Time: 3 Hours                                                    Max Marks: 100

Part A: Law of Treaties (25 marks)

1. Explain the Vienna Convention on the Law of Treaties.
2. What is pacta sunt servanda?
3. Discuss treaty interpretation principles.
4. What are reservations to treaties?
5. Explain treaty succession.

Part B: International Organizations (35 marks)

6. Describe the structure of the United Nations.
7. What is the Security Council? Discuss its powers.
8. Explain the role of the General Assembly.
9. What is the World Trade Organization?
10. Discuss regional organizations like the EU and AU.

Part C: Contemporary Issues (40 marks)

11. Explain international environmental law.
12. What is the law of the sea?
13. Discuss cyber law and internet governance.
14. Explain international refugee law.
15. What is international trade law?
