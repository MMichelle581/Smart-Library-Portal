International Law HSS F252 - Exam Paper 1
Time: 3 Hours                                                    Max Marks: 100

Section A: Foundations of International Law (30 marks)

1. Define international law and explain its sources.
2. What is the role of the United Nations in international law?
3. Explain the concept of state sovereignty.
4. Describe the principle of non-intervention.
5. What are treaties? Discuss their formation and termination.

Section B: Human Rights Law (35 marks)

6. Explain the Universal Declaration of Human Rights.
7. What are civil and political rights?
8. Discuss economic, social, and cultural rights.
9. Explain the European Convention on Human Rights.
10. What is the International Criminal Court?

Section C: International Disputes (35 marks)

11. Describe methods of peaceful settlement of disputes.
12. Explain the role of the International Court of Justice.
13. What is international arbitration?
14. Discuss the use of force in international law.
15. Explain humanitarian intervention.
