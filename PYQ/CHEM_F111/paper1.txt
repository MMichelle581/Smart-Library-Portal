Chemistry CHEM F111 - Exam Paper 1
Time: 3 Hours                                                    Max Marks: 100

Section A: Short Answer Questions (10 x 2 = 20 marks)

1. Define the concept of atomic orbital and explain its significance.
2. What is electronegativity? Explain the periodic trends.
3. Describe the structure of benzene and explain resonance.
4. Compare ionic bonding with covalent bonding.
5. Explain the concept of oxidation and reduction reactions.
6. Define molarity and molality with examples.
7. What is Le Chatelier's principle? Give an example.
8. Describe the different types of chemical bonds.
9. Explain the concept of pH and its importance.
10. What are catalysts and how do they work?

Section B: Long Answer Questions (4 x 10 = 40 marks)

11. Discuss in detail the periodic table and its organization. Explain the trends in atomic radius, ionization energy, and electron affinity.

12. Explain the various theories of chemical bonding including Lewis theory, Valence Bond theory, and Molecular Orbital theory.

13. Describe thermodynamics principles as applied to chemical reactions. Include discussions on enthalpy, entropy, and Gibbs free energy.

14. Analyze the kinetics of chemical reactions. Explain rate laws, reaction mechanisms, and the factors affecting reaction rates.
