Chemistry CHEM F111 - Exam Paper 3
Time: 3 Hours                                                    Max Marks: 100

Part I: Conceptual Questions (40 marks)

1. Explain quantum mechanical model of atom.
2. Describe the molecular orbital theory.
3. What is VSEPR theory? Explain with examples.
4. Discuss thermochemistry and Hess's law.
5. Explain colligative properties of solutions.

Part II: Application Problems (30 marks)

6. Apply Le Chatelier's principle to industrial ammonia synthesis.
7. Calculate buffer solution pH.
8. Determine reaction order from experimental data.

Part III: Advanced Topics (30 marks)

9. Discuss coordination chemistry and crystal field theory.
10. Explain spectroscopy principles and applications.
11. Analyze organic reaction mechanisms.
