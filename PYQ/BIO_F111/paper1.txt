BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus
Biology BIO F111 - Exam Paper 1
Time: 3 Hours                                                    Max Marks: 100

Section A: Cell Biology (30 marks)

1. Describe the structure and function of cell membrane.
2. Explain the process of cellular respiration.
3. What is mitosis? Describe its phases.
4. Compare and contrast prokaryotic and eukaryotic cells.
5. Discuss the structure of DNA and RNA.

Section B: Genetics (30 marks)

6. Explain Mendel's laws of inheritance.
7. What is genetic mutation? Describe different types.
8. Discuss the central dogma of molecular biology.
9. Explain gene expression and regulation.
10. Describe DNA replication mechanism.

Section C: Ecology and Evolution (40 marks)

11. Explain Darwin's theory of natural selection.
12. Describe different types of ecosystems.
13. What is biodiversity? Discuss its importance.
14. Explain food chains and food webs.
15. Discuss population dynamics and growth models.
